# AdvisorFlow AI - Product And Pitch Draft

## 1. Working Title

AdvisorFlow AI

## 2. Tagline

From scattered client signals to governed advisor action.

## 3. One-Sentence Pitch

AdvisorFlow AI helps insurance and financial advisors know which client to contact first, what to say next, which AAG or ASG partner to involve, and what compliance guardrail to check before action.

## 4. The Problem

Advisors manage many signals every day: policy reviews, missed premiums, client life events, business changes, partner opportunities, CPD needs, expenses, and compliance requirements.

The information is usually spread across systems and personal memory. A CRM may store the record, but it does not always tell the advisor what matters now, what action to take, and what risk to avoid.

This causes:

- Slower client follow-up
- Missed high-value opportunities
- Inconsistent partner referrals
- Manual admin effort
- Late compliance checks
- Higher dependence on advisor memory

## 5. The Solution

AdvisorFlow AI is a daily command centre that converts client context into clear action.

It provides:

- Morning advisor brief
- Priority client ranking
- Client memory
- AI-style client summary
- Next-best actions
- Follow-up draft composer
- Partner desk matching
- CPD recommendations
- Consent and compliance guardrails
- Admin dashboard and audit trail

## 6. Why It Is Different

AdvisorFlow AI is not only a dashboard. It connects four layers in one flow:

1. **Productivity**
   - Advisors know who to focus on and why.

2. **Growth**
   - Partner referral opportunities are detected and tracked.

3. **Learning**
   - CPD suggestions are tied to real advisor/client situations.

4. **Governance**
   - Consent, suitability, compliance, and audit controls are visible before action.

## 7. Demo Story

The demo begins with an advisor starting the day.

AdvisorFlow AI shows that several signals arrived overnight. The system ranks clients by urgency and value. Mr. Tan appears as a high-priority client because of a liquidity event, estate planning opportunity, and coverage gap.

The advisor opens Mr. Tan's profile. The AI Copilot explains the opportunity, shows evidence, and recommends actions. The Action Composer drafts a compliant follow-up. Partner Radar recommends the ASG Tax Advisory Desk and AAG Estate Concierge with reasons and evidence needs.

Next, the advisor selects Mr. Kumar to show a different type of value: service-risk prevention for a missed premium and debt exposure.

Then the advisor selects a consent-locked client. The system masks private details and blocks follow-up or referral creation until consent is refreshed.

Finally, the demo switches to Admin. Admin can see managed premium, referral pipeline, compliance queue, pending expenses, admin review controls, and audit logs.

## 8. Judge Wow Moments

- The system does not just show data; it ranks action.
- Recommendations include reasons and evidence.
- Partner matching connects advisor work to AAG x ASG business units.
- Consent-locked clients prove privacy and compliance thinking.
- Admin gets an operating view, not just a static report.
- The prototype works offline without API keys, making the demo reliable.

## 10. Production Vision

In production, AdvisorFlow AI would connect to a secure database, authentication, and approved AI services. Advisors would only access their own clients. Admins would see review queues and audit logs. AI suggestions would remain advisor-approved, explainable, and logged.

The long-term product becomes a governed advisor operating system for productivity, growth, learning, and compliance.

## 11. Closing Line

AdvisorFlow AI helps AAG x ASG advisors move faster without losing control: the right client, the right action, the right partner, and the right guardrail in one place.
